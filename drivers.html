DRIVERS

The following people are the official drivers for Safe Ride Syosset. To be approved as a driver for Safe Ride Syosset is a thorough process. These drivers are carefully selected by the Safe Ride Syosset CEO and his advisors due to their character, maturity, and dedication to community service. The following young men and women volunteer their weekend nights to join the fight against teenage drinking and driving. They all are licensed drivers who have made the commitment to be a drug free and sober driver for the program. During the night, they are mandated to have a partner with them. Most importantly, all Safe Ride Syosset drivers have signed a waiver promising not to drive drunk, nor recklessly, nor in any manner to endanger the welfare of their passengers. The community of Syosset owes these drivers a great deal of gratitude for their selflessness and for helping to keep our children and roads safe.
