ABOUT US


Safe Ride Syosset was founded in September 2011 by Joshua Lafazan. Safe Ride Syosset is a not for profit organization whose goal is to help avoid Syosset teens from getting into a car with a drunk driver.  Safe Ride Syosset offers transportation home for all Syosset High School students by a sober and drug free driver.

Safe Ride Syosset’s hotline, 516-375-4697, is available Friday and Saturday nights, from 10PM until 2AM.  Syosset High School students, working in pairs, who have been selected as Safe Ride drivers, will be dispatched to pick up the student or students who called the hotline and will take them to their respective homes, free of charge.

People of all ages make the bad decision of getting in a car with a drunk driver.  Syosset students often make this choice because they do not want their parents to find out that they had been drinking themselves or were at a function where drinking and drugs were used.  SRS offers these students an easy choice to remain safe without parental involvement.


To clarify, Safe Ride Syosset is NOT a free taxi service.  Calling Safe Ride Syosset for a free ride home is like calling for an ambulance without need.  It takes away an important resource for those that really need it.  Safe Ride Syosset is a not for profit organization, run exclusively by volunteers dedicated to combating teenage drinking and driving in Syosset.  Please feel free to use our services when you are in need.  Call or text the hotline when needed.  You MUST unblock your number for a response.

 

 

If you have any questions or comments, please feel free to email us at Josh@saferidesyosset.org
