FAQ'S


Who is eligible to use Safe Ride Syosset?
-Current Syosset High School students are eligible to use the program.


What are the hours of operation for Safe Ride Syosset?
-Safe Ride Syosset is available Friday and Saturday nights, from 10 p.m. until 2 a.m.


Where will Safe Ride Syosset take these students?
-Safe Ride Syosset will take students to their respective homes.


How much does it cost to use the Safe Ride Syosset?
-Safe Ride Syosset is a free program.


Is Safe Ride Syosset affiliated with Syosset High School or any other organizations?
-No. Safe Ride Syosset is a completely independent organization.


How does a Syosset High School student contact Safe Ride Syosset when in need of a ride?
-The student can call the hotline, tel:516-375-4697, using an unblocked number.


Who are the drivers for Safe Ride Syosset?
-Current Syosset High School students who are licensed drivers. 


How are these drivers audited?
-All Safe Ride Syosset drivers must sign a waiver promising to be drug free and sober and to behave in a safe manner at all times. Their parents have also given written consent for them to participate as a volunteer.


Do these drivers drive kids home alone?
-No.  All drivers are mandated to have a partner in the car with them.


Who is funding Safe Ride Syosset?
-No one.  Safe Ride Syosset is a completely voluntary non-profit organization.  All drivers volunteer their time on the weekends, as well as money for gas.


Are parents involved with Safe Ride Syosset?
-No.  Safe Ride Syosset is a program run strictly by students for students. 


Is a Syosset High School student who is out of town eligible to use Safe Ride Syosset that night?
-No.  Safe Ride Syosset will only pick up Syosset High School students within the Syosset School District geographic area.


Who is in charge of Safe Ride Syosset? 
-Joshua Lafazan is the CEO of Safe Ride Syosset. 


How can I submit questions/comments I have?
-You can email Josh@saferidesyosset.org with any questions/comments you might have.
