GET INVOLVED

I am a Syosset student.  How can I get involved?

If you are a Syosset High School student, you can get involved in many ways.  You can spread the word about Safe Ride Syosset so more people know the service is available.  You can use the service and become part of the solution rather than the problem of teenage drunk driving. If you are a licensed driver in New York State, you can request to become a driver with Safe Ride Syosset.  You can email Josh@saferidesyosset.org with your request.

I am a Syosset parent.  How can I get involved?

If you are a parent in the Syosset School District area, you can get involved in many ways.  You can spread the word about Safe Ride Syosset so more people know the service is available.  You can make sure your children have Safe Ride Syosset’s hotline  tel: 516-375-4697 number in their contact list so they can always contact us when needed.  Most importantly, by talking to your kids about the dangers of drinking and driving and how fatal that decision can be, you are helping to become part of the solution, rather than the problem of teenage drunk driving.

I am a student that does not reside in Syosset.  How can I get involved?

There are many ways for you to get involved.  The easiest and simplest way to get involved is to never drink and drive.  If all people stopped drinking and driving, 10,000 people wouldn’t die this year- one person every 50 minutes (www.MADD.org).  The next way you can get involved is to volunteer to be the designated driver for your friends.  Kids will resort to drinking and driving when there is no easy alternative available to them.  By being the designated driver, you are sacrificing a night of inebriation and brain cell loss to keep the streets of your community safe.  This action is an act of selflessness and heroism.  The biggest way you can get involved is by starting a Safe Ride program in your town.  Safe Ride Syosset is available to assist in the expansion of community involvement for this worthwhile endeavor.  If you have any questions or need any advice please feel free to email Josh@saferidesyosset.org

I am a parent that does not reside in Syosset.  How can I get involved?

The simplest and easiest way of getting involved is to donate to Mothers Against Drunk Driving (most commonly known as MADD), an organization whose goal is to “aid the victims of crimes performed by individuals driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs, to aid the families of such victims and to increase public awareness of the problem of drinking and drugged driving” (MADD.org).  Most importantly, by talking to your kids about the dangers of drinking and driving and how fatal that decision can be, you are helping to become part of the solution, rather than the problem of teenage drunk driving.
